About our Project

This project is to support frequent riders of the city's transportation system, we want to offer a space to verify your itinerary, get an update on a delay or share feedback on a specific network. A quick search on this site could help you time trains and buses near to your location, manage commutes and facilitate the transit experience. Why would people use an additional transit tool? Having a space to plan trips, commutes and transprotation with community feedbacks, live search updates, and less information overload.
